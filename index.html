About Bite Me!

Welcome to Bite Me!, the ultimate haven for Engenes and non Engenes and waffle lovers alike! Born from a passion for electrifying music and tasty waffles, our shop is a place to connect, unwind, and share your love for your favorite idols.

We play the latest hits and classic bops, host fan events, and serve up delicious waffles named after your favorite songs and idol. Bite Me!, A sweet connection worth sharing.

ENHYPEN's Eat Me Up!

Sweet Venom

Inspired by the allure of a dangerous delight. Our Sweet Venom Waffle features a rich, dark cocoa-infused waffle, generously drizzled with chocolate, finished with a dusting of cocoa powder.

Dark Moon

Embrace the deepening connection with our Dark Blood Waffle. This deep chocolate waffle, as captivating as a lunar eclipse, is topped with luscious strawberry that will make you feel like your on top of the moon.

Future Perfect

A waffle masterpiece, perfectly crafted to steal the spotlight. The Future Perfect Waffle is our ultimate creation, a waffle generously spread with luscious, creamy Biscoff spread and topped with crushed Biscoff cookies for an irresistible crunch.

Bite Me (Signature Eat me up)

Our shop's namesake and a taste of destiny. The Signature Bite Me Waffle invites you to experience connection at first bite. This golden, perfectly crisp waffle is generously slathered with rich, warm Nutella, then topped with fresh, perfectly ripe banana slices. It's a sweet, comforting, and utterly irresistible classic made unforgettable – a truly fated flavor you'll crave.

No Doubt

"When there's no doubt about what you crave, our No Doubt Waffle delivers pure, sweet satisfaction. This classic, fluffy buttermilk waffle is loaded with fresh, vibrant strawberry slices and generously drizzled with a sweet strawberry jam.

Polaroid Love

Capture a moment of pure comfort with our Polaroid Love Waffle. This beautifully golden, fluffy waffle is served simply, yet perfectly, with a dollop of creamy butter and a generous pour of golden maple syrup. It's a sweet, comforting embrace, just like a cherished memory..
